About SoundSense

Voice authenticity checking and interview practice in one place.

What is SoundSense?

SoundSense helps you verify whether an audio clip sounds like a real human voice or an AI-generated one, and gives you structured feedback on interview answers. Use it to spot potential deepfakes or to practice and improve your interview responses.

AI Voice Check

Upload an audio file or record live to analyze the voice for authenticity.

  • Upload or record — Support for WAV, MP3, and OGG. You can also record directly in the browser.
  • Acoustic analysis — The system checks multiple voice features (e.g. prosody, spectral and energy patterns) to estimate how human-like the voice sounds.
  • Result — You get a label (e.g. Human / AI-generated), a confidence score, risk level, and plain-language advice on what was checked.

Interview Coach

Record or upload an interview answer and get feedback on clarity, confidence, and structure.

  • Optional question — You can enter the interview question (e.g. “Tell me about yourself”) so feedback can be more targeted.
  • Upload or record — Same audio options as AI Voice Check.
  • Evaluation — The system transcribes your answer and scores multiple criteria (e.g. clarity, confidence, structure, relevance). You see an overall score, strengths, and areas to improve.

History

All your analyses are saved so you can review them later.

  • Stats — Quick counts for voice checks and interview evaluations, plus how often AI was detected in voice checks.
  • List — Past voice checks and interview sessions with dates. Use “View” to open the full result (scores, transcript, advice).
